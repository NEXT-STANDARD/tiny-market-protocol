## Why This Exists

Between 2025 and 2035, three forces are reshaping capital markets:

1. **Solopreneurs are achieving 5.7× revenue-per-employee** via AI tooling
   (AI-native startup avg: $3.48M/employee vs traditional SaaS $610K/employee).
2. **Traditional VC is collapsing under the DPI crisis**
   (2018-vintage funds sitting at ~0.6× DPI vs historical 0.8×).
3. **IPO markets have shrunk to a fraction of their 1990s size**
   (US: median 158/year in 1980-2019 → 90 in 2025, with median IPO age 13-14 years).

The existing capital stack was built for **a world of 200-person companies
scaling to 2,000**. The next decade's winners will be **tiny teams scaling to
massive revenue without ever adding headcount**.

They need a market designed for them.

This repository outlines what that market could look like.

## The TINY / AGORA Distinction

This repository explores two complementary ideas:

- **TINY** — A listing venue with radical criteria (≤10 employees, ARR ≥ $500K,
  profitable, no VC > $10M). A stock exchange where smallness is the virtue.
- **AGORA** — An agent-native trading layer where human investors express
  *intent* and autonomous AI agents execute 24/7/365.

They can exist separately. They can compose. This protocol specifies both.
