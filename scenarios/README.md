# Scenarios

This directory contains speculative narrative accounts of how the TINY /
AGORA transition could unfold.

Scenarios are **not forecasts**. They are imagined dispatches from
plausible futures, written to make the abstract specifications in `/aips`
concrete — so that non-technical readers (founders, investors,
regulators, journalists) can feel the shape of the future the AIPs
describe.

## Contributing New Scenarios

Scenarios are CC0 and welcomed. Write yours and open a PR.

Suggested angles that have not yet been written:

- **Scenario from the failure case** — What does 2030 look like if this
  transition does *not* happen?
- **Scenario from outside Japan/US** — Seen from Lagos, São Paulo,
  Mumbai, Jakarta, Seoul.
- **Scenario from the VC survivor** — A frontier-capital fund that
  pivoted and thrived.
- **Scenario from the regulator** — A day inside a financial-supervision
  agency in 2029.
- **Scenario from the principal** — First-person account of living
  entirely on intent-based investing.
- **Scenario from the agent** — Written from an agent's perspective
  (carefully, to avoid anthropomorphization traps).
- **Dark scenario** — What does systemic capture of this model look
  like?

## Style Guidelines

Scenarios work best when they:

- Use real organizations, cities, and systems. Fiction grounded in
  reality is stickier than pure fiction.
- Clearly disclaim fictional portrayals of real individuals.
- Avoid utopia. Include failure modes and costs.
- Use specific numbers, dates, quotes — even when invented, specificity
  is more credible than vagueness.
- Show, don't explain. A leaked memo > a summary of what the memo meant.
